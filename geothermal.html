Geothermal Energy

  1. Geothermal is a one of idea to produce electricity by using thermal energy (energy which is made by transferring heat energy to electricity) which is made by using the crust heat from the earth.
  2. The word geothermal comes from the Greek words geo (earth) and therme (heat).
  3. Geothermal energy is a renewable energy source because heat is continuously produced inside the earth by core of the earth and transferred to crust by conduction.
  4. People use geothermal system to generate electricity.
