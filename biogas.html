Biogas

  1. Biogas is a combustible mixture of gases
  2. Chief constitute of biogas is Methane

WHY BIOGAS:

  1. It is a Renewable Source of energy
  2. It has a High calorific value i.e. it produces more energy when 1Kg of it is burnt than many other fuel.
  3. It is a Clean fuel
  4. It is a Useful byproduct

BIOGAS PRODUCTION TAKES PLACE IN 3 STAGES:

  1. Hydrolysis and Acidogenesis
  2. Acetogensis
  3. Methanogensis

FACTORS IN BIOGAS PRODUCTION:

    Basic Factors:

  1. bacteria
  2. food
  3. contact

    Environmental Factors:

  1. temperature
  2. pH
  3. toxicity
